About

Temples are special places for members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Each one is engraved with the words "The House Of The Lord," and some of our most sacred worship takes place there. I try to highlight the natural beauty of the temples, while depicting them as real and accessible places that we can and should visit regularly. I would be honored if you choose to purchase any of my photos for display in your home or to give as a gift.
